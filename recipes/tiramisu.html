Tiramisu recipe

Description

The tiramisu come from Italy.

It's a simple cake made with speculos, cream and coffee.

Ingredients

  • 7 eggs
  • 2*boudoirs
  • 1*speculos
  • 150g sugar
  • coffee
  • 500g mascarpone cheese
  • vanilla
  • chocolate powder

Steps

  1. Separate the yellow from the eggs
  2. Make the white part as snow
  3. Chake the sugar, vanilla and mascarpone together
  4. Put the white egg in and chake it slowly on the same direction
  5. Put speculos as a base and add cream on it
  6. Take boudoirs soak it a little bit with coffe and put it upon the cream
  7. Add cream on it and now to the same but soak more the boudoirs with coffee
  8. Continue like this until you reach the top of your box and now put all the cream
  9. Add the chocolate powder on it
  10. Let the tiramisu rest on the fridge for minimum 5 hours
  11. Enjoy !
